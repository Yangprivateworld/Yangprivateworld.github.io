When I was trapped during the pandemic, I will always look at the google map and image travelling around the world, but it was until 2023 when I had chance for my world travel, but everything had changed completely compared with pre-pandemic era.

Berkeley in California

I stayed in Berkeley, California as a visting student in University of California. Berkeley is a harmonious and tranquil town with diverse ethnicities. Everybody I met here was very kind and amiable, and honestly speaking, I was deeply impressed by how such a mixed crowd of people can live in such a peaceful way. California is really a magic space.
